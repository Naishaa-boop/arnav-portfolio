The Photographer

Presence
over
perfection.

Arnav is a photographer drawn to real, unfiltered moments — whether it's the stillness of nature, the unpredictability of wildlife, or the quiet expressions of people.

His work isn't about staged perfection. It's about capturing presence — the kind that makes you feel like you were there.

Now expanding into travel and spaces, his approach remains the same: observe deeply, shoot honestly, and tell stories that stay.
